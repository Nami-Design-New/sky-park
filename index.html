about us

There’s nothing like a day at SkyPark, experience excitement and challenge in our 4500 sq.2 park in Sodic West. Reservations are not required, so come out on your own or with friends and family – ages 5 to 101

Look up – you’ll see a series of platforms interwoven throughout the poles connected by cable, wood, rope and zip lines to form different elements. Each element presents a different challenge of how to get to the next platform. SkyPark equals a total of 4 different courses with over 50 unique elements, creating a series of adventures to lead you throughout the Park.
